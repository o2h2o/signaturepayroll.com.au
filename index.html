ANZ Payroll Email Blunder Sparks Industry Concern

September 25, 2025 Industry News

ANZ Bank faced intense scrutiny after automated emails mistakenly informed employees of termination before formal communication. The incident highlights critical risks in automated HR processes and the importance of human oversight.

Key Takeaways for Payroll Professionals:

  • System safeguards must prevent premature communications
  • Human oversight remains critical for sensitive decisions
  • Clear protocols for redundancy processes are essential

AI and Employment: Challenging the Doomsday Narrative

May 20, 2025 Technology

David Sacks argues that AI fears are overstated, emphasizing that workers are more likely to lose jobs to AI-proficient colleagues than to AI itself. The rise of "agentic AI" presents new productivity opportunities.

Implications for HR & Payroll:

  • Understanding AI tools is no longer optional
  • Upskilling and continuous learning are crucial
  • AI augments rather than replaces human capabilities

Australia's "Right to Disconnect" Expansion

New legislation strengthens employees' rights to disconnect from work communications outside business hours, with significant implications for payroll and HR practices.
